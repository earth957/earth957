## Gist
Pyaw (ပြော — "to speak") is a Burmese-first English learning web app. It guides Burmese speakers through everyday English with simple lessons, vocabulary, and Burmese-language context built in from the start.

## Story
My relative has been wanting to improve their English for work and daily life, but mainstream apps like Duolingo feel overwhelming when the entire interface is in English. I built Pyaw so they can learn from a familiar, comfortable starting point — their own language — and build confidence step by step without getting lost.

## Why
Most English learning tools are designed for a global audience and assume some baseline English proficiency. Burmese speakers are underserved. Pyaw fills that gap with a clean, minimal UI and Burmese-language guidance, making the learning curve feel manageable instead of discouraging.
